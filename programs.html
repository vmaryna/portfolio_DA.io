Programs

2024 - 2025


"Bootcamp" program

March 2025 - till now

The "Powercoders" Bootcamp is a full-time flagship program with supported job integration. It includes a 13-week intensive programming course with dedicated specialization tracks (Web Design, Advanced JavaScript, Java, Testing, DevOps, etc.) and a subsequent internship at an IT department of a company in Switzerland.


"Access Fast Track" program

November 2024 - January 2025

The program was launched by the "Capacity" organisation. The aim of the program is to support highly qualified migrants and refugees in their professional integration. "CONNECT Networking Event" of the 11-th Cycle was hosted by Johnson&Johnson company in January 2025.

"Women Connect" program

June - July 2024

The "Women Connect" program was launched by the "Power Coders" company and helps women, coming from a migrant or refugee background, to consider a career in IT and to succeed in the exciting and dynamic field of IT. The June-july 2024 "Women Connect" sessions were hosted by the "Avaloq" company and have comprised 16 keen participants.
